ABOUT

 
 
            Digital Espresso is the epitome of a modern day coffee house. 
            The seemingly simple cafe started in the humble abode
            of founder and coffee-lover Juliana Ong the year 1952;
            decades after, the small shop has transformed into a well-loved cafe,
            boasting of exquisite blends, curated flavors, and fruity undertones.
            Digital Espresso takes pride in the nation's farmers and is proud
            to source its hand-picked coffee beans from Mindanao's mountain farms.
